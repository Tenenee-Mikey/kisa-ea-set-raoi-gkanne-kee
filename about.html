Teraa KISA?

KISA Bon te rabwata are ea ikotia te nati n I-Kiribati n koaua aika reirei i USP Fiji n boutokaira, karikarekeira ao buokira i marenara.

Committees

Iai ana taibora KISA are ena tauraoi n buokii ngkami te nati n Kiribati aika kam reirei i USP. Nakai naba ake ana tauraoi n kabira KISA ao n tei ibukin KISA man ana waaki n tibanako USP. Aia details KISA committees ena kona nb n reke ikai.

Cultural Events
